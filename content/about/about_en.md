# zhiyanzhaijie (脂砚斋介)

A developer from Chengdu, China, as well known as the hometown of panda.

My open-source philosophy is this: what humans create can outlast them, and Open source **should be** a pure one.

I received a bachelor’s degree in architecture from South China Agricultural University in 2022. In the same year, I taught myself programming and became a web developer, with about four years of professional experience.

I believe the web still holds tremendous open-source potential in the future. My current focus is applying WebAssembly in the web ecosystem.
You can refer to my recent open-source project, [Best of RS](https://bestofrs.org).

## The purpose of this blog

Following my open-source philosophy, this blog is meant to clarify:
